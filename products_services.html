Our Products & Services

Luxury, Heritage & Interior Design at Your Fingertips

Project Collection

Our collection merges craft and storytelling. Each cupboard and sofa is designed as a functional sculpture — rooted in African artistry, shaped by premium materials, and made to transform interiors into galleries of everyday life.

Sofas & Lounge Chairs — Sculptural Comfort

The Tigerwood Armchair

Commanding presence meets comfort. This armchair blends the raw strength of tiger-like patterns carved into rich wood with plush cream cushions. Its broad, sculptural arms make it not just a seat but a throne for modern living spaces. A piece that roars with personality while holding onto elegance.

The Twin Tiger Set

Designed for both intimacy and drama, this set of two tiger-patterned chairs with a matching ottoman is perfect for conversational corners. The deep wood frame grounds the design, while the fabric evokes the wild beauty of untamed landscapes. Luxurious, yet inviting — a pair that turns any room into a private lounge.

The Ivory Crest Sofa

This rounded armchair embodies softness and strength in perfect balance. Covered in creamy fur-like upholstery with subtle animal markings, it feels like a blend of modern luxury and primal design. The deep seat invites relaxation, while the bold shape makes it a sculptural centerpiece.

The Outdoor Safari Loungers

For the outdoors but designed like art. These lounge chairs carry the same bold tiger-print design as the twin indoor set, but reimagined for open-air living. Built for relaxation under the sun, they celebrate nature with patterns that honor the wild — making your garden or patio an extension of luxury.
